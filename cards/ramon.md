# Ramon - Throw Archetype

Ramon is an Earth-centered deck that pressures through throws and half-block damage. He can force damage through defensive decks, but his attacks are slower and he has limited draw.

## Character

### Ramon, Lucha Star
- Type: Character
- Hand Size: 6
- Vitality: 29
- Symbols: Earth / Life
- Tags: Lucha / Freestyle
- Enhance: Your Throw attack gets +2 damage.
- Response: Once per turn, after your Throw attack deals damage, ready 1 foundation that was committed to play an ability this Enhance Step.
