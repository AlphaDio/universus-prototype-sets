# Lin - Assassin Poison Archetype

Lin is a Death-centered Assassin deck that places poison counters on the rival and converts them into inevitability. He can tax resources and deal incremental damage, but his direct damage output is lower than the other archetypes.

Poison counters: During the End Phase, each player loses 1 vitality for each poison counter on them, then removes 1 poison counter from themselves. Poison counters are not damage.

## Character

### Lin, Hizoku Assassin
- Type: Character
- Hand Size: 6
- Vitality: 26
- Symbols: Death / Evil
- Tags: Assassin / Poison
- Response: Once per turn, after your Assassin attack deals damage, give your rival 1 poison counter.
- Enhance: Remove 1 poison counter from your rival: Your Assassin attack gets +2 speed and +2 damage.
